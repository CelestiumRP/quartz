Any player who can chat can change their expression without having to .charsel

/express angry  
/express grin  
/express kind  
/express neutral  
/express sad  
/express serious  
/express smirk  
/express tired  
/express upset  
/express very-sad

To turn off automatic expressions:

/express off

To turn them back on again:

/express on
